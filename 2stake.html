Stake with us!

We are a german community run radix validator node with a 0% fee. Our aim is to contribute to a decentralized financial system. The node runs on a 24/7 monitored ESXi Cluster with automatic backups and zero downtime. To optimize updates and possible downtimes, we always have a backup node up and running.

Early stakers will receive airdrops of our native \$RDT token! The earlier and the more you stake, the more \$RDT tokens you get. Formula for Airdrops:

$$\frac{\sum stake_{user} \times \sum Airdrop}{\sum stake_{validator}}$$
