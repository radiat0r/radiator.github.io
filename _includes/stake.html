why stake with us?

We are a german community run radix validator node with 0% fees. Our aim is to contribute to a decentralized financial system. The node runs on a 24/7 monitored ESXi Cluster with automatic backups and zero downtime. To optimize updates and possible downtimes, we always have a backup node up and running. Early stakers will receive airdrops of our own native token! We are working on some dapp ideas, which will be announced soon - stay tuned!
